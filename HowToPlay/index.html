Overview

Before the game starts, you will be assigned a role, either crewmate or impostor. The main objective as a crewmate is to finish your tasks or vote off all the impostors. While the impostors main objective is to kill crewmates until the number of impostors is the same as the number of crewmates, or the crew doesn't complete a sabotage in time. If an impostor kills you, you become a ghost. While your a ghost, you're still responsible for finishing your tasks. And don't forget, even if you die you can still win!

Crew

As a crew member, you will be playing detective while trying to finish your tasks. Tasks are given to at the start of the game and can be seen on the left side of your screen. You can also check which tasks you have yet to do by looking at the places highlights in yellow on your map.

Impostor

As an imposter, your job is to murder people until there is the same amount of impostors as there are crew members. The goal of an impostor is to ultimately kill others and find ways to not only prove your innosence but to try and frame peple who are truly innocent.

Voting

When a body is found or when someone calls an emergency meeting, everyone still alive in the game will be called to a voting session. This is where everyone can converse and share what they know to try to figure out who the impostor is. The meeting starts off in a discussion phase, where no voting is allowed, then progresses to the voting phase, where you choose to vote on someone suspicious or skip the vote. And please, don't vote just because someone says "sus".

Tasks

Tasks are given to every crew member at the start of the game. One of the main objectives of the crew is to complete their all their tasks, doing so would result in a crew win. While maps do share tasks, there are some tasks that are unique to each map. Tasks are a great way to verify fellow crew mates as well as to catch imposters faking them. For more info, check the wiki.

Sabotage

As an imposter, you have the ability to sabotage people. At the bottom right of your screen there is a sabotage button, and when pressed, will bring up a map with a bunch of different icons (see buttons to learn more). Although sabotages are a great way to get out of a sticky situation or get an easy kill, they do have cooldowns, so use them wisely.

Life After Death

Once you die in the game, you become a ghost. Even though your technically dead, your still required to do your tasks if the crew hopes to get a task win. If you are in a voice chat, you will have to stay muted for the rest of the game, and the only other people you can talk in text chat are other dead people.
